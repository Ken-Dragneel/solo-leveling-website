shadow Monarch A.K.A

SUNG JIN WOO

(성진우)

Sung Jin-Woo is the protagonist of Solo Leveling. He was previously known as the World's Weakest Hunter and delved down the path of becoming the World's Strongest Hunter he was Reawakened and became a Player.

Jin-Woo was reawakened as a Player after surviving the Cartenon temple and completing the secret quest "courage of the Weak". The mysterious System has allowed Jin-Woo to limitless level up and increase his strenght, unlike all hunters, who are unable to increase their strenght unless reawakened.
